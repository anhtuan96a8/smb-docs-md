# The Disabled Employees List Item

## **Item Description**

This Item manages the list of disabled employees who are working in the enterprise.

## **Implementation instruction**

* Percent: the percentages of disability.
* From…to: the disabled time, if disability for life, the **To date** column can be blank
* Approval: the regime confirmation is disabled employees.
