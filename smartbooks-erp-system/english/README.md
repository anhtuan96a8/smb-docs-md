# SMARTBOOKS ERP PROGRAM

## INTRODUCTION

ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) is a software system that helps an entire business running effectively, supporting automation and processes in finance, human resources, manufacturing, supply chain, services, procurement, and more. The widespread application of ERP makes it become a brain of a business. Multiple areas of a business and their processes are connected together, enabling the flow of the data between them.

As the development of global ERP market, S\&S ERP gives your business a simple but effective solution to manage its core process. There are 9 main modules including 4 modules for manufacturing **(Sales Order, Purchase Order, Inventory Management and Manufacturing)**, 2 modules for financial statement **(Accounting Report and Customer Portal)**, a module for HR (HR & Payroll Report) and 2 modules for setting up **(Master Data and Setup Tools)**. All modules have the interfaces together and S\&S ERP connects all the linkages so that the dataflow can go through every single department to the financial and management reports.

·     **Sales Order**: Orders from customers from quotations/ price lists to delivery notes and return (if any), linking to AR (Accounting Receivable) of Accounting.

·     **Purchase Order**: Orders to vendors from price lists to receipt notes and good return (if any), linking to AP (Account Payable) of Accounting.

·    **Inventory Management:** Stock movement and balance including stock input/ output, stock transfer or stocktaking.

·     **Manufacturing**: Manufacturing orders from BOM to finished good receipt note and repair (if any), the variance between the actual and planned material quantity.

·     **Accounting Report**: Interface between ERP and Accounting data, financial reports and production reports.

·     **Customer Portal**: Visualization for the health of business.

·     **HR & Payroll Reports**: Linking with the HR software, general information about the employees, timesheet and salary.

·    **Master data**: List of customers, vendors, items, warehouse and so on.

·   **Setup Tool**: Company information, permission for the users and configuration values.

This user guide provides the instructions of Purchase Order, Sales Order, Inventory and Manufacturing only. Other modules have its own user guides, please contact S\&S team to receive the full set.

_**Status**_

·     **Open**:  open a form, detail changeable (edit/copy/delete)

·     **Processing**: on the process, detail unchangeable.

·     **Completed**: process finished, detail unchangeable.

·    **Hold**: the voucher is held, not linked to the accounting yet, detail changeable

·     **Release/ Posted**: the voucher is release, linked to accounting, detail unchangeable.
