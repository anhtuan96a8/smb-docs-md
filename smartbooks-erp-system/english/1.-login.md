# 1. LOGIN

When you register our system, you are given the user accounts (one or many depended on the agreement) with User, Password and Company code. Go to the Log in page and fill in these information. Choose the preference language and Sign in

<figure><img src=".gitbook/assets/image (64).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

On the Home page, there are Company name, Tool, Language, Username, Guideline and Logout on the top right.

·     **Tool**: select the fiscal year and the currency to review the reports

·     **Language**: select/ change the display languages

·     **Username**: identify the login users, related to the module and function permission (if any)

In order to open the vertical menu bar, click ≡ icon on the top left of the screen to start using ERP modules
