# 2.2 Company information

To change the company information, go to **Menu -> Setup Tools -> Company Information**

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (68).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Fill in or change the information. The titles which are not given are set as blank in the reports.

·     **Company code**: automatic setup on the system. No change.

·   **Image**: upload company logo or branch symbol.

·     **Other info**: available to adjust or renew.

·     **Save.**
